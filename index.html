Our Mission

The National Juneteenth Pageant exists to celebrate the history of Juneteenth while developing confident, educated, and service-minded young women who are prepared to become tomorrow's leaders.


Contestants participate in leadership development, community service, interviews, talent presentations, public speaking, cultural awareness, and scholarship opportunities while proudly representing their communities across the nation.

What We Celebrate

More than a pageant — a leadership experience rooted in culture, education, service, and excellence.

👑 Leadership

Develop confident leaders through mentorship, public speaking, professional development, and civic engagement.

📚 Scholarship

Encourage academic achievement while providing scholarship opportunities for participants nationwide.

❤️ Community Service

Promote volunteerism and meaningful community impact through service initiatives and outreach.

🎭 Cultural Excellence

Celebrate African American history, culture, creativity, achievement, and the significance of Juneteenth.
